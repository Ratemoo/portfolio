# Editable Portfolio

This portfolio now blends two references:

1. A cyber-tech personal portfolio feel
2. A warmer editorial layout with richer typography, numbered sections, and more narrative project cards

## Edit your content

Open [content.js](C:\Users\george ratemo\OneDrive\Dokumente\New project\content.js) and update:

1. `theme` for colors
2. `site` for title, hero labels, intro notes, and section support text
3. `hero` for your main headline, image, summary, and stats
4. `about` for your bio, quote line, skills, experience, and education
5. `skills` for the marquee list and craft cards
6. `services`, `timeline`, `projects`, and `gallery`
7. `contact` for your email, phone, location, and social links

## Replace images

Swap any file inside [assets](C:\Users\george ratemo\OneDrive\Dokumente\New project\assets) with your own image using the same filename, or point `content.js` to a different local file.

## Open the site

Open [index.html](C:\Users\george ratemo\OneDrive\Dokumente\New project\index.html) in your browser.
